01 — THE 5AM GAP THE MORNING WINDOW
01

Most routines die in the first ten minutes.

The alarm rings. You are barely awake and entirely unguarded — and that is precisely when the snooze does its quiet work.

Discipline isn't won at the gym or the desk. It's won in the narrow window between the alarm and your feet hitting the floor. Lose that window and the whole day tilts.

Shoshin lives in exactly that window. It doesn't ask you to be motivated. It just won't let you drift back.
