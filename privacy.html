Introduction

Welcome to Shoshin. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience on our app.

Key Commitments

  • No Ads: Shoshin does not contain advertisements
  • No Data Sales: We never sell your personal data
  • Your Control: You can delete your data anytime
  • Transparency: Full disclosure of what we collect

1. Information We Collect

  • Phone number (for authentication)
  • Profile information (name, picture, bio)

  • Device and usage information
  • Group and social sharing data

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide app services and features
  • Authenticate your account
  • Sync data across devices
  • Calculate and display streaks

  • Support group features

3. How We Store Your Data

Your data is stored:

  • Locally on your device (works offline)
  • In Firebase Cloud (encrypted, US servers)
  • Protected with HTTPS and AES-256 encryption
  • Geographically redundant for backup

4. Your Rights

  • Access your data anytime (Profile → Settings → Download My Data)
  • Delete your account and all data (Profile → Settings → Delete Account)
  • Control privacy settings in-app
  • Opt-out of communications

5. Who Can Access Your Data

  • You: Full access to all your data
  • Group Members: Can see your public posts and streak only
  • Shoshin Team: Access only for technical support
  • Google/Firebase: As cloud service provider
  • Third Parties: We do NOT share your data

6. Children's Privacy (Ages 13-17)

  • Shoshin is designed for ages 13+
  • Parental or guardian consent required for ages 13-17
  • We do NOT serve targeted ads to minors
  • We do NOT share personal info of minors with third parties
  • Contact: cobwebtechnologies1@gmail.com for child privacy concerns

7. Data Security

We protect your data using:

  • In Transit: HTTPS encryption and secure SSL/TLS
  • At Rest: AES-256 encryption
  • Administrative: Limited employee access, all logged

8. Data Retention

  • Active Accounts: Data kept while account is active
  • Inactive Accounts (12+ months): May delete after notification
  • User Deletion: All data deleted within 30 days

9. Do Not Sell My Personal Information

We do NOT sell, rent, share, or trade your personal information for any purpose, including marketing. This applies to all users.

Subject: "Privacy Request" or "Privacy Concern"
